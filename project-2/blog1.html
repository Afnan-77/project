Sports

Date of publication: Nov 11, 2021

    Just as diet is essential for a healthy body, exercise is vital for the body, mind, and psyche. And it helps significantly in raising the level of results he wants to get, such as losing weight, increasing body health, and others. Sport has many benefits, including:

  • It helps promote heart health and improve blood flow to the body, which helps resist many diseases such as high blood pressure and protect it from heart attacks, strokes, and atherosclerosis.

  • They are reducing fat, lowering cholesterol levels, losing weight, improving digestion and bowel movement in the body.

  • As for mental health, it improves mood, promotes relaxation, and modifies the air.



As for what should be taken care of by those who practice sports:
  • Do not exercise immediately after eating and wait an hour and a half to two hours before starting it.

  • It is necessary to do the warm-up and gradual exercises so as not to cause injuries, as well as the extension after the completion of the training.

  • sure to drink water and fluids, and there is a break between exercises.

  • Appropriate clothing and shoes for sports.
